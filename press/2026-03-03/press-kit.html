Albuquerque's First Luxury Head Spa
Opens Its Doors March 6, 2026

Liora Head Spa brings an elevated, fully customized scalp and hair ritual to the Southwest

ALBUQUERQUE, N.M., March 6, 2026. Liora Head Spa, Albuquerque's first luxury head spa, officially opens today at 8000 Paseo del Norte Blvd NE, Suite C9. Founded by Ashley Trebitowski, Liora introduces a new category of wellness to New Mexico, one rooted in intentional touch, scalp health, and the belief that genuine care flows from scalp to soul.

Unlike traditional salon services, every Liora ritual is fully customized. Each visit begins with a personalized consultation and scalp assessment, followed by a treatment tailored specifically to that guest's scalp and hair needs. The spa's philosophy is simple: no two guests are the same, and neither should their care be.

"Liora is a name meaning 'my light' or 'I have light,'" said founder Ashley Trebitowski. "Everything we do here is designed to bring that light back through intentional touch, premium products, and a genuine human connection that you can actually feel."

The spa offers six signature rituals ranging from the 45-minute waterless Stillness Ritual ($110) to the growth-focused Renewal Growth Ritual ($230), which incorporates high-frequency comb therapy and red-light LED treatment. All services utilize R+Co professional products alongside proprietary Liora-exclusive formulations developed specifically for the spa. Every ritual includes a complimentary consultation, scalp assessment, and access to a self blow-dry styling space.

For guests committed to ongoing scalp wellness, Liora offers three monthly membership tiers starting at $115, including a limited Founding Membership locked at that rate for life for the first 25 members. Gift cards, branded as the Gift of Light, are also available for purchase.

Liora Head Spa is open Wednesday through Saturday, 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Sunday, 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Appointments can be booked via the website, phone, or email.

About Liora Head Spa

Liora Head Spa is Albuquerque's first luxury head spa, offering elevated scalp and hair treatments focused on relaxation, scalp health, and overall well-being. Services are fully customized and never one-size-fits-all, beginning with a personalized assessment for each guest. The spa emphasizes human connection, creating intentional rituals that support a scalp-to-soul connection and are designed to bring guests back to their light. All services utilize high-end, professional-grade products selected to support both scalp health and hair integrity.

Meet the Founder
Ashley Trebitowski
Founder & Owner
Ashley
Trebitowski
Founder, Liora Head Spa

Ashley is a wife, mother of three, and lifelong beauty and wellness enthusiast who is thrilled to introduce Liora Head Spa to Albuquerque. Liora, inspired by a name meaning "my light," offers a luxury head spa experience focused on customized scalp and hair rituals designed to restore balance, encourage circulation, and nurture each guest's natural radiance. Every service is personalized, never one-size-fits-all, and crafted to bring clients back to their light through intentional touch and meaningful connection.

Although she attended cosmetology school, Ashley discovered her true passion in massage and esthetician-focused work, especially in helping people feel genuinely cared for and relaxed. For the past 14 years, she and her husband have run Pixegon, a software company they built together. In addition to her tech career, she owns a gourmet s'mores food truck and is active in real estate investing.

With Liora Head Spa, Ashley brings together her love for beauty, wellness, and intentional client care to create a serene space where clients can unwind, reset, and experience deep relaxation from scalp to soul.
